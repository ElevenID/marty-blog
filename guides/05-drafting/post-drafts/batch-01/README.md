## Why this batch belongs together

These three posts create the cleanest opening path currently available in the Marty editorial system:

- first, explain why identity should be treated as protocol infrastructure rather than a metered platform category
- second, give readers the stable five-primitives map they can use to stay oriented across the publication
- third, prove that one of the hardest architectural pressures — privacy — is handled through explicit policy rather than verifier habit

Together, they establish the category thesis, the system model, and the first hard constraint.
