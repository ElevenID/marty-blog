## Why this batch belongs together

This batch turns Marty's deployment and buying model into something operationally and commercially legible:

- first, show why per-check verification pricing punishes successful systems and why protocol infrastructure changes the cost curve
- second, give operators and platform teams a practical lens for choosing between hosted, self-hosted, and edge runtimes
- third, make offline verification honest by spelling out cache setup, freshness decay, and failure-mode policy

Together, these drafts move the publication from architectural confidence into runtime and budgeting realism.
